More than half of Gateway City downtowns are immigrant-serving

Of the 25 Massachusetts Gateway City downtowns, 14 (56%) are located in census tracts where at least 30% of the population is foreign-born.
14 immigrant-serving
11 not immigrant-serving
Source: U.S. Census Bureau, ACS 2022 5-Year Estimates (B05001, B05002). Threshold: ≥30% foreign-born at census tract level.  REUTERS GRAPHICS

Excluding Boston, more than half of downtowns are still immigrant-serving

Of the 24 remaining Gateway City downtowns, 13 (54%) meet the 30% foreign-born threshold.

11 not immigrant-serving
